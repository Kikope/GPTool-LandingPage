About Us

Simplifying AI Access

GPTool is dedicated to simplifying AI interaction. We integrate GPT into WhatsApp, making the power of artificial intelligence easily accessible to all. Our mission is to empower users to effortlessly engage with AI, bridging the gap between technology and convenience.

Empowering Conversations with GPT Integration

How it Works?

Using AI with GPTool is as simple as sending a message on WhatsApp. Just type your query, and our platform will promptly deliver informative responses powered by AI. We've prioritized simplicity to make AI accessible in your WhatsApp chats, ensuring its potential is easily within reach for everyone.

Is there any cost?

GPTool is an independent, currently non-profit project with a mission to bring accessibility to those who find text generative tools challenging in the current context. The service is free but has a limit. To keep the project running, we incur monthly expenses based on usage. This is why there is a monthly limit of 50 free messages per person. If you wish to use GPTool beyond this limit, you can contact us to request more usage for a small fee. We also welcome donations to support the project, and your name will be forever recorded as a contributor to the GPTool.

What's Up Next?

Currently, we're focusing on text generation. But we have exciting plans to enhance our capabilities in the future. Stay tuned for updates on upcoming features, including image generation!
